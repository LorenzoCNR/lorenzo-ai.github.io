Neuro-Bridge – Representation Learning for Brain-to-Brain Coupling

Neuro-Bridge is a modular research platform for modeling high-dimensional neural signals using deep learning and geometric representation learning. It includes contrastive embedding architectures, transformer-based encoders for temporal neural sequences, and custom decoding modules for cross-condition generalization and representation stability analysis.

Python ·PyTorch · Contrastive Learning · Transformer · Representation Learning · Neural Signal Processing

Systemic Risk & Financial Econometrics

Quantitative modeling of financial instability using Bayesian tail-risk estimation, regime-switching dynamics and network-based contagion structures. Focus on stress scenarios, volatility spillovers and early-warning indicators derived from both market data and textual macro-financial signals.

Bayesian Inference · Markov Switching · DCC-GARCH · Tail Risk Modeling · Text-based Risk Signals

Geopolitical Risk Index & Conflict Escalation Modeling

Construction of a geopolitical risk index using news frequency analysis, structural VAR identification and zero-inflated models to estimate conflict escalation probabilities.

NLP · Structural VAR · Zero-Inflated Logit · Markov Chains

Advanced Policy Evaluation & Geographic RDD

Causal inference using Geographic Regression Discontinuity Design, matching estimators and quasi-experimental methods applied to institutional development and macro-policy evaluation.

RDD · Matching · Causal Inference · Stata · MATLAB · QGIS
